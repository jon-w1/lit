Dunamis Connections

Empowering individuals to reach their
potential through connecting resources.

We believe that each individual has a huge potential to impact this world and was created with a unique purpose.
We exist to empower you to reach your potential! You are a tremendous resource and we want to connect you with opportunities to meet needs around the globe that only you can meet. You will be empowered in the powerful process of empowering others.

Join us today to change lives and let your life also be transformed!

Empowering Teams

Be part of our trips! We organize teams to work alongside the locals of developing nations to build lasting relationships and changes. We have opportunities in: agriculture, construction, education, medical, ministry, and technology.

Empowering Nations

We take individuals into nations to aid in local community projects. This partnership teaches the natives a trade and help them to empower their communities to be self-sustained. We are empowering the nations, one community at a time.
